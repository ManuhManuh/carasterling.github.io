Orienteering

This is a VR experience, brought to you by VentR-out, that is meant to "Extend the Outdoors". It features orienteer racing with a map and compass, alone or as a team.

I was originally contracted to take care of the backend stuff, specifically PlayFab and Azure. I ended up doing that, and quite a bit more.

I have learned an incredible amount from working on this project:

  • Using Azure for server side APIs
  • Working with a team of up to 10 people from four continents
  • Having to stick to someone else's design: when working on solo projects or in pairs, it's really easy to fall back on "if at first you don't succeed, redefine success". When you have to stick to a design, you just have to figure out the hard stuff.
  • Integrating SDKs: This project uses many, and sometimes they fight...
  • How to make orienteering maps!
